# Problem Statements — ApplyNXU (persona-grounded)
> Persona: **Amaka**, a working professional in Lagos, applying for the MBA on her phone. Mobile-first, emerging-market, non-technical. Each statement is tied to App start → App complete.

## PS1 — Sign-up dead-end (C1)
- **I am:** Amaka, applying for the MBA from my phone after work.
- **Trying to:** create my account and start the application.
- **But:** I fill in everything and "Register Now" just says *"An error occurred. Please try again."* — with no idea what's wrong.
- **Because:** the form let me pick my country for the phone number, but the backend silently rejects it.
- **Which makes me feel:** confused and distrustful — if step one is broken, is this school even legitimate?
- **Final:** *Amaka needs sign-up to accept the country the form offered her, because a silent generic error at registration makes her abandon at the widest, highest-intent point of the funnel.*

## PS2 — The identity wall (F6/F8/F9 + F1)
- **I am:** Amaka, on my phone, minutes from finishing my application.
- **Trying to:** submit my ID and a selfie so I can complete and pay.
- **But:** my ID is a PDF scan (rejected), my photo is a 12MB HEIC (rejected), it wants a plain-background selfie and front+back — and all of this before I've paid a cent.
- **Because:** the upload accepts only small JPG/PNG images, and heavy identity verification sits before payment.
- **Which makes me feel:** stuck and frustrated — I did everything else and now a file-format wall stops me.
- **Final:** *Amaka needs to submit whatever ID photo she already has, and not clear a heavy KYC gate before she's committed, because format/size rejections plus pre-payment verification drop her at the highest-intent step.*

## PS3 — The finish-line payment (USD-only)
- **I am:** Amaka, ready to pay the $25 application fee.
- **Trying to:** pay and complete my application.
- **But:** the only options are USD (my Nigerian card is blocked for international/USD).
- **Because:** there's no local currency/rail.
- **Which makes me feel:** so close yet blocked — I want to give them my money and the form won't let me.
- **Final:** *Amaka needs to pay in a way her Nigerian bank allows, because USD-only checkout blocks conversion at the very last step.*

## Context & constraints (all three)
Mobile-first · slow/expensive data · non-English national ID common · low tolerance for friction (applying after work) · trust is fragile (is this US school real?).
